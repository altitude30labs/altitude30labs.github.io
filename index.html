About Altitude 30 Labs

Altitude 30 Labs is a spatial intelligence company transforming aerial and satellite data into actionable insights.

We integrate high-resolution aerial platforms, LiDAR, hyperspectral and multispectral sensors with advanced AI-driven analytics to help organizations map, monitor and manage both natural and built environments with precision.

Our expertise spans precision mapping, water resources management, vegetation & crop health analysis, infrastructure inspection, coastal and flood monitoring, marine and atmospheric sciences, climate change and environmental change detection.

We process raw sensor data into calibrated, georeferenced products and deliver insights through custom dashboards and automated reporting systems.

Technology stack: UAVs & manned aircraft, LiDAR, hyperspectral & multispectral imagers, photogrammetry, AI-driven image analytics, and cloud-native geospatial pipelines.

Whether you need rapid incident response mapping or long-term monitoring programs, we turn airborne and spaceborne data into trusted intelligence.

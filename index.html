About Altitude 30 Labs

Altitude 30 Labs is a remote sensing company delivering actionable intelligence from air and space. We combine high-resolution aerial platforms, LiDAR, hyperspectral and multispectral sensors with advanced geospatial analytics to help clients map, monitor and manage natural and built environments.

Our core services include precision mapping, vegetation & crop health analysis, infrastructure inspection, coastal and flood monitoring, and environmental change detection. We process raw sensor data into calibrated, georeferenced products and deliver insights through custom dashboards and automated reporting.

Technology stack: UAVs & manned aircraft, LiDAR, hyperspectral & multispectral imagers, photogrammetry, AI-driven image analytics, and cloud-native geospatial pipelines.
